iOCO Heritage Day Game 2024

Celebrate South Africa's Diversity!

Join us for an exciting game that explores the rich cultural heritage of South Africa. Test your knowledge, learn new facts, and have fun with colleagues!

How to Play:

  1. Choose your difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  2. Answer questions about South African culture, history, and iOCO.
  3. Complete fun challenges and storytelling prompts.

  5. Race against the 10-minute timer to earn as many points as possible!
